About FINF

FINF Pte Ltd is a cutting-edge trading company that deals with an extensive range of natural foods and food ingredients. We focus on the development of new food formulas, food ingredient technology & application and ever-changing functional foods trends.

FINF’s headquarters & main sales office is situated in Singapore. In addition, our warehouses located in Thailand, Malaysia, Philippines and Hong Kong help to facilitate our international outreach.

Our company’s mission and purpose is to provide customers with the highest level of product standards and tailored technical support at competitive market prices. We aim to offer the best and most complete options for your needs.
